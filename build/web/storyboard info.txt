Users type and their role:
- The system will have two users, which are HEP and Counselor.
- Hep role is to log new disciplinary incident and assign them to the counselor.
- Counselors role is to manage appointment schedules and session completion status.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CRUD

CREATE -
- HEP can log or add new discipline incident, they need to provide students info, discipline offense details and assign to counselor.
- Counselor can set appointment date for cases with no date

READ -
- HEP can also read the data in dashboard page, where they can see total cases, 
  common offenses, pending cases and also charts of offense and success rate
- HEP can read the recorded discipline in page record E-Disiplin page, this will show all the data of recorded disciplinary incident.
- Counselor can read stats in dashboard page, like total, complete and pending cases they handle. also pie chart an latest cases.
- Counselor can see table of records of the cases that they handle.

UPDATE -
- HEP can update or edit the recorded data of disciplinary incident, for example students info, incident info or assign to other counselor.
- counselor can change status of case, for example when they set date it also change status to pending, 
  and also can change status to complete after the date of appointment.

DELETE -
- HEP can delete the recorded data by clicking the delete button in record E-Disiplin page.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trends - 
- Hep dashboard is to view total disciplinary cases, most common offenses, pending counseling sessions 
  and also pie charts of cases by Offense Type and line chart of counseling Session Success Rate.
- Counselor dashboard is to view total, completed and pending cases that theyve been assigned for. and also completion rate by percentage 
  and pie chart of case by status.
